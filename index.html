A little background

What is FIRST?

For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology (FIRST) is an international nonprofit organization that inspires young people to pursue oppurtunities in STEM. FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a robotics league run by FIRST for grades 7-12. Students design, build, and program a robot to compete against other teams. They are pushed to build their robots with engineering principles in mind. FIRST recognizes teams who excel in creative thinking, design, community outreach, and the scientific process.
